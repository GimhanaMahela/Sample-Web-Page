Our Services


Behavioral Consultation & Counseling

Soul Dogs, Inc. employs many behavior/mental health therapists with a variety of expertise. We are all trauma-informed therapists and specialize in this area. We are available to teams, families, and providers to assist in completing an assessment and developing a behavior support plan to benefit the individuals we serve. We are here to help you help your consumer. This is in addition to the counseling with the individuals.

Animal Assisted Therapy

Soul Dogs, Inc. provides trauma-informed, evidenced based practices utilizing animals as a piece of our counseling interventions to support the health and wellness of our clients

Specialized Day Habilitation

An onsite program four days/week is provided on site so trainers/participants may teach the dogs the necessary skills in the specialized day habilitation program. Our goal is for each participant to attain his/her/their maximum functional level or to be supported in a way allowing him/her/them to gain an increased level of self-sufficiency. We believe dogs are a vital piece for these individuals to feel safer and less alone so they may better access the community.

Supported Employment

Soul Dogs Inc. provides supported employment services which are individualized for our clients and their specific needs. Supported employment is comprised of 3 different programs: Job Readiness, Job Development and Job coaching to meet our clients where they are.

Mission

Soul Dogs, Inc. provides behavioral, habilitative, rehabilitative, and professional development services to individuals with intellectual, developmental, and physical disabilities and autism.

Vision

Our vision is to provide ongoing documentation and evidence of the power of the human-animal relationship in healing trauma.
We focus our services for those in the IDD system. However, we are an inclusive program and welcome individuals from the community to participate in our classes. We hope this therapy will be one day be funded by states providing IDD services.

Our Standing

We are a nonprofit (501c3) company seeking new ways of assisting IDD individuals heal and feel sage being part of their communities.
Success with veterans is well-researched and documented. We propose that service/assistance/emotional support/ therapy dogs, in conjunction with the quality therapy and wrap around services, can offer similar success for the IDD community.
